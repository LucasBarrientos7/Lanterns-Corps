INDIGO TRIBE


Power Source: The Indigo Light of Compassion.
Entity: Proselyte, a being similar to an indigo colored octopus or cephalopod.

The Indigo Tribe, also known as the Indigo Lantern Corps, are the wielders of the indigo light of compassion. Their ranks are made up of merciless killers who were forced to wear rings of Indigo to make them feel compassion.
