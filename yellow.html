SINESTRO CORPS


Power Source: The Yellow Light of Fear.
Entity: Parallax, a giant yellow insect-like being.

The Parallax entity is a space parasite that is the embodiment of fear that was imprisoned within the Central Power Battery on Oa. After a long time, the entity became known as the yellow impurity, the cause for the Green Lantern Corps' weakness with the color yellow.
