BLUE LANTERN CORPS


Power Source: The Blue Light of Hope.
Entity: Adara, a being similar to a huge blue bird.

Their power rings are charged by the emotion "hope," and they greatly increase the strength of any nearby Green Lanterns. Their home is the planet Odym and their representative entity is Adara. Ganthet and Sayd were their original leaders, and their greatest champion is Saint Walker.
